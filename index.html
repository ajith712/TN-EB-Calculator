Hello visitors,This is to calculate the ELECTRICITY BILL as per the Tamilnadu Government tarrif plans,This is very helpfull to check your eb bill at your end every month,if you know the units consumed,you'll calculate the bill directly by mention the units in the first box mention as (To Calculate Amount With Reading Units) but if you don't know the units consumed,find the units in the second box mention as (If You don't know the Units, Calculate the Units) by inputing a last bill Reading and current month reading,This is very usefull to tracking your EB units and amount everytime you want.Note : This is applicable to (LOW CONSUMABLE PLACE such as) Home.If you want to check for High Consumable Places click the menu option on the top and select check for Bill Check For High Consume.
